Data Security & Privacy
How RhetorIQ handles your data
Processing
RhetorIQ processes all inputs exclusively via the Anthropic API — one of the most rigorously audited AI infrastructures available. No content entered into RhetorIQ is stored on external servers, sold to third parties, or used to train AI models.
Session Isolation
Each session is isolated: data entered in one client workspace is never accessible to another. Your inputs are processed in real time and treated as strictly confidential.
Legal Compliance
Lorena Lienhard operates RhetorIQ in strict compliance with Swiss data protection law (DSG) and the European General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).
Confidentiality Standard
Sensitive communications — merger announcements, performance reviews, internal strategy documents — are treated with the same confidentiality as a privileged advisory relationship. What you enter stays between you, RhetorIQ, and Lorena Lienhard.
